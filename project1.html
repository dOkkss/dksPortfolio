Case01 / TAGEförskola / YRGO assignment

The aim of this design was to enhance ad navigation and streamline the process of inserting ads into the service. This was achieved by implementing UI changes, making it quicker and more efficient with fewer clicks required to accomplish the same goal.

Product design
UX research
User interviews

Overview

Background

The background is a project where we were tasked with coming up with a concept related to recycling or reusing. I chose to focus on preschools, as it's a cause I'm deeply passionate about after working in the field for over 15 years. I was aware from the start that there was a similar concept in Gothenburg City, but I wanted to make it easier for those working in preschools to use the service. The primary focus was to reduce the time it took to post ads and make it simpler to book them.

Objective

During my interviews, a consistent sentiment emerged: dissatisfaction with the previous design and a general frustration with its usability. There was a unanimous desire for an actual app to enhance day-to-day usage. Therefore, my focus in the design process was to streamline ad insertion and improve its visual appeal.
